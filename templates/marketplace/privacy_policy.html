Privacy Policy

At Victoria Marketplace, we value your privacy. We collect only necessary information (e.g., name, address, payment details) to process your orders and improve our services. We ensure authentication and enforce Cross-Site Request Forgery (CSRF) tokens on all critical operations within the app, like adding items to cart and making payments. This eliminates the risk of malicious transactions without the consent or knowledge of our esteemed customers and sellers.

Your data is encrypted and not shared with third parties without consent. For more details, contact us at support@victoriamarketplace.com.
